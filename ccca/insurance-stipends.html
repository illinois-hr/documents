Insurance Stipends

Who is eligible for an insurance stipend?

A unit can provide an insurance stipend to an employee who is NOT eligible for our State of Illinois Employee Group Insurance Program (SEGIP) and/or where RSL BasicCare coverage does not meet the minimum requirements for certain visa holders. Benefit information can be found at https://www.hr.uillinois.edu/benefits.

How much can I provide for an insurance stipend?

Each unit or college may determine its own scale…

  • $1500 for employee only
  • $2500 for employee plus one dependent
  • $3000 for employee plus two or more dependents
